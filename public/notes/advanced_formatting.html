Advanced Formatting

The settings provided in this section allow for a more control over the prompt building strategy.

For Pygmalion models

Disable description formatting

NAME's Persona: won't be prepended to the content your character's Description box.

Disable scenario formatting

Scenario: won't be prepended to the content your character's Scenario box.

Disable personality formatting

Personality: won't be prepended to the content your character's Personality box.

Always add character's name to prompt

Doesn't do anything (Included in Pygmalion formatting).

For non-Pygmalion models

Disable description formatting

Has no effect.

Disable scenario formatting

Circumstances and context of the dialogue: won't be prepended to the content your character's Scenario box.

Disable personality formatting

NAME's personality: won't be prepended to the content your character's Personality box.

Always add character's name to prompt

Appends character's name to the prompt to force model to complete the message as a character:
